GOLDEN CHOPSTICKS

A simple food dream

Golden Chopsticks, at BKC, Mumbai, is a restaurant that aims to take its diners on a culinary journey like no other. It is Chef Sampurnaa Debnath's food dream to serve authentic Chinese that is compatible with the Indian palate at the very heart of Mumbai for every food enthusiast. Serving the best of Oriental Chinese cuisine with the choicest of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, this simple restaurant located at JioWorld, BKC, Mumbai is the first of a dreamed to be franchisee.
